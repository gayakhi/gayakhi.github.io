The Best Brownie Recipe

Brownies Cockaigne from "The Joy of Cooking" by Irma S. Rombauer

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4oz. unsweetened chocolate
  • 4 room temperature eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sifted all purpose flower

Instructions

  1. Grease a 9 x 9 inch pan with butter and sprinkle cocoa powder on top
  2. Preheat oven to 350C
  3. In a double boiler, melt the butter and chocolate together. Cool this mixture
  4. In a separate bowl, beat your eggs and salt until they are light in color and foamy
  5. Gradually add in the sugar and vanilla, and continue to beat until well creamed
  6. With a few swift strokes, combine the cooled chocolate mixture into the eggs and sugar. Fold by hand until it is uniformly colored
